Target Labs Took Part in the Technology Conference & Expo 2012
by TargetLabs Inc. posted 12/14/2012

Target Labs, Inc. took part in the Technology Conference & Expo 2012 between December 5 and 6 at Walter E. Washington Convention Center. This is one of DC's biggest IT events of the year. The conference included coaching sessions, thought-provoking speakers, an experienced and helpful Expo Hall, and plenty of networking opportunities.

The event was organized by the American Society of Association Executives, which represents more than 21,000 association executives and industry partners from over 10,000 organizations. ASAE members manage leading trade companies around the world, individual membership societies and voluntary organizations whose reach is global.
